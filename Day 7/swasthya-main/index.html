About us

Swasthya(means health in english) is a niche blog that cover health topics, related content of the health industry and the general community. HELPING NGO

HelpAge India

HelpAge India has one of the largest mobile healthcare programs across India, providing free healthcare services to destitute elders.

Rural Health Care Foundation

founded by the late Arun Nevatia. It strives to provide low cost primary healthcare to the lowest strata of the socio-economic pyramid and is doing well in achieving its goals.

Goonj

Goonj address the basic but neglected issues of the poor and values the traditional wisdom of the local people. It enables them to participate in Goonj’s solutions and gives out urban material as reward

Deepalaya

HIt promotes self-reliance, with prime attention to women and children. It also works towards development of the urban and rural poor population

Lepra Society

As the name suggests, Lepra Society works to empower people affected with leprosy. It also fosters the healthcare of victims of lymphatic filariasis.

CRY(Child Rights and You)

CRY(Child Rights and You) was started by Rippan Kapur in 1979 with six of his friends and fifty rupees at his mother’s dining table. They had a dream of witnessing a day when every single Indian child would enjoy his/her rights such as survival, protection and development.
