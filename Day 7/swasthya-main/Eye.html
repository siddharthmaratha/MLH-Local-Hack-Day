More than 4.2 million Americans aged 40 years and older are either legally blind. The leading causes of blindness and low vision in the worlds are primarily age-related eye diseases such as age-related macular degeneration, cataract, diabetic retinopathy, and glaucoma. Other common eye disorders include amblyopia and strabismus.

COMMON EYE PROBLEMS INCLUDE:

  1. Eyestrain [tiredness in the eyes]

  2. Refractive Errors [short-sightedness, farsightedness, distorted vision] - Uncorrected refractive error is a preventable cause of visual impairment in India.

  3. Dry eye syndrome - Tear glands are not able to make enough tears.

  4. Night Blindness [Nyctalopia] - Difficulty to see in low light.

  5. Conjunctivitis [Allergic / bacterial / Viral] - It is an inflammation of the conjunctiva.

  6. Amblyopia - It is reduced vision in one eye due to vision development disorder.

  7. Eye floaters - Spots [strings, rings, cobwebs] in the vision.

  8. Blepharitis - Inflammation of the eyelid causing crusty /dry eyelid, stickiness, grittiness.

  9. Cataract: A cataract is an opacity within the clear lens of the eye.

  10. Glaucoma - It is a condition where increased intraocular pressure may result in damage to the optic nerve.
